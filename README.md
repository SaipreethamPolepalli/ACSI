# Hidden Crates

An exciting, casino-style high-stakes gambling game where players test their luck by opening warehouse crates. Avoid the explosives, rack up massive multipliers, and decide whether to cash out early or risk it all for the ultimate jackpot!

## 🎮 Game Features

*   **Dynamic Multiplier Scaling:** The fewer crates left on the board, the higher your potential payout. 
*   **Customizable Betting:** Full control over your risk with adjustable wager amounts and a variable number of total crates to play with.
*   **Risk vs. Reward Mechanics:** Enter the **Profit Zone** after opening enough crates to break even, then choose between a secure payout or chasing a **60x Jackpot**.
*   **Top Net Wins Tracker:** Keeps track of your highest multi-crate streaks and biggest payouts in real time.
*   **Bankruptcy Protection:** Ran out of funds? The loading dock boss has your back with a quick reclaim button to get you back in the action.

## 🛠️ How to Play

1. **Set Your Risk:** Adjust the **Crates Amount** slider and input your desired **Wager Amount**.
2. **Start the Round:** Click **Start Round** to lock in your bet.
3. **Search the Warehouse:** Click on the crates grid to uncover hidden yields.
4. **Cash Out or Push Your Luck:**
   * Hit an explosive? **Busted!** You lose your active wager.
   * Feeling satisfied? Hit **Cash Out** to secure your current earnings and credit them to your Net Worth.
